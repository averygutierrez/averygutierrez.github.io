3D Print of Minecraft Sculpture


"Minecraft Sculpture" Created with Minecraft in ART 74 || 854x480

This art work was created in Minecraft and was 3D Printed using mineways and Cura. This work represents gaming culture and presents nostalgia from my childhood. Like many others I spent my time playing Minecraft when I was younger. Creating this art piece in one of my childhood games and bringing it to life displays bringing my inner child to life again.





"Minecraft comes to life" Created with Minecraft, Mineways, Cura, 3D printer, and Acrylic paint in ART 74 || 854x480

This is the finished result of 3D printing using mine ways, cura, and adding details to the physical piece. The scale of this 3D print is similar to the scale of the virtual pigs within the game. The details of the acrylic paint added to the 3D print brings the piece to life by giving the piece color and a glossy finish, and allows a digital piece to be brought to reality.
