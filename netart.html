NetArt


"Ode to Odi" Created with Illustrator, Photoshop, and Textedit in ART 74 || 612x792

My NetArt is based on my experiences with my first childhood pet. I created this piece in the form of a story to convey the resemblance to a poem book. My intention is to create a sense of nostalgia and connection with the viewers through this piece about the commonality of fond memories we have of our first childhood pet.
