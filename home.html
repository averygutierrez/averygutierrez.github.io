Stay Home


"Stay Home" Created with Illustrator and Photoshop in ART 3 || 787x570

This piece is a recreation of The Large Bathers by Paul Cezanne. This work represents people avoiding social distancing regulations by still traveling, celebrating spring break, and not wearing a mask. The repetition of the words on the tree trunks and the doctor figure in the upper right corner fading into the sky while everyone avoids staying 6ft apart represents how many people would choose a moment of having fun in big groups while putting others at risk as doctors and nurses spend endless hours helping those in need than staying home and keeping everyone including themselves safe.
