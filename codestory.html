Coded Story Book


"You are the Sun" Created with Illustrator, Photoshop, Audacity, and Textedit in ART 74 || 792x576

The message of this story focuses on the struggles women face in society and is a reminder that despite the misogyny that occurs in society you are not what or how society perceives you and you are not alone in your struggles. I wanted to incorporate various symbolisms for the messages so I decided to use the sun instead of a human head to represent that women are still bright, bold, and strong despite these things that occur. The sun usually represents masculine energy as well but I wanted to display that women are able to break free from these labels that society puts on them. The glitches represent how these hurtful words are used to tear women down but as you read through the story you will notice that the glitches disappear and the background colors become brighter. All these things symbolize the strength women have and how they are able to overcome these struggles. My Intended impact on the audience is to remind women that they are not alone in there struggles and that other people will see this and be moved to end misogynistic tendencies and actions.
